Overview
DOORS is a Roblox horror experience developed by LSPLASH and RediblesQW. It is known for its randomly generated rooms, unique entities, and tension-based survival gameplay. Players progress through a series of numbered doors, each room potentially containing puzzles, traps, or hostile entities that must be avoided to survive.
Gameplay Summary
Players explore an endless hotel-like complex composed of procedurally generated rooms. The goal is to reach Door 100 and escape while surviving encounters with entities such as Rush, Ambush, Seek, Screech, and Figure.

The game emphasizes visual and audio cues—listening for footsteps, flickering lights, or breathing can be the difference between life and death. Tools such as lighters, vitamins, lockpicks, and crucifixes help players survive the journey.
Notable Floors

Entities
Some of the most recognized entities include:
• Rush — Destroys light sources and instantly kills players in its path.
• Ambush — Similar to Rush but returns multiple times.
• Seek — Chases players through scripted corridor sequences.
• Figure — Blind, but detects movement and sound.
• Screech — Appears in dark rooms and attacks if not looked at quickly.
• Queen Grumble, Giggle, and Gloombats — New creatures introduced in Floor 2: The Mines.
Trivia
• DOORS has surpassed over a billion plays since its release.
• The game uses a combination of atmospheric audio design and low-light visuals to build tension.
• The developers often hide cryptic teasers about upcoming floors in updates and badges.
• Its visual presentation and entity design have influenced many Roblox horror developers.
